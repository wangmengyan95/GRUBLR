##########################################################################################################
http://localhost:8000/ is the page for login and register.
When you register a new user, yo will receive a simple email confirmation.
##########################################################################################################
http://localhost:8000/findPassword is the page for users who forget their password and want to find it.
In this page, you can input your username and submit the form. The system will automatic generate a new password and send it to your email address. You can use the new password to login the system and revise your password.
When you test the program, please use your real email address to register. Because the system will send the new password to your email address.
##########################################################################################################
http://localhost:8000/home is the main page.
You can input the Grumblr in the text area and post it by clicking Grumblr button under the text area.

You can upload an image with your grumblr by click the button under the text area.

You can click the tag under the textarea to see your and your friends' Grumblr, your friends' Grumblr and your Grumblr.

You can type the key word in the search inputField on the navigation bar to search Grumblrs. If you block some other users, their Grumblr will not appear in the result.

You can click the shut off icon on the navigation bar to log off the website.

You can click the Expand link to replay a Grumblr.

You can click the thumbUp icon to support a Grumblr.

You can find strangers on the sildebar, by clicking the follow button, you can view his personal page. You can follow, unFollow, block, unBlock this users in his personal page.
##########################################################################################################
http://localhost:8000/profile is the page for login user to view and edit his profile.
I also used ajax in this page. You can click the Edit button to edit your profile and upload your profile picture.

You can find strangers on the sildebar, by clicking the follow button, you can view his personal page. You can follow, unFollow, block, unBlock this users in his personal page.
##########################################################################################################
http://localhost:8000/changePassword is the page for login user to change his password.
I also used ajax in this page. You can input your old password, new password and confirm password. After the system verify the old password is correct, the system will change your password.
##########################################################################################################
http://localhost:8000/relationships is the page for login user to view his relationships with other users
You can see the users you followed, the users who folloed you and the users who you block by clicking the link under the profile picture.
